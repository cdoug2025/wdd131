What Is Chess?

Chess is a fun and popular 2-player strategy game. You can grab a friend to play, or go to one of many places online to play with other people. The rules are pretty simlpe, and the goal of the game is to checkmate your opponents king. Once you learn how the pieces move, you can play a game as long as you have a board and pieces. Chess is fun at a beginner level, but there are also people who aim to master the game. Overall, chess is a quick way to have fun with other people!
